How This Works

Each set gets a Value Score (0-100) based on four things:

  • Pack price — cheaper packs score higher
  • Best card value — more valuable chase cards score higher
  • Pull odds — better chances of pulling the top card score higher
  • Chase card density — more good cards in the set scores higher

Where Prices Come From

  • Sealed product prices — Dawnglare (TCGPlayer market data), updated daily
  • Card values — manually tracked from TCGPlayer/eBay sold data
  • Pull rates — community data from PokeBeach, CardChill, pack opening studies
Pull rates are community estimates, not official. Prices change daily. This is a guide, not financial advice.
