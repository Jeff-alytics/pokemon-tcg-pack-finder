Score Breakdown

Methodology

The Pack Value Score is a 0–100 composite heuristic ranking sets by how efficiently your money converts into high-value pulls.

Score = w₁ × CostEfficiency + w₂ × ChaseMagnitude + w₃ × PullOdds + w₄ × HitDensity

Components

  • Cost Efficiency — Lower market price per pack scores higher. Uses minimum across TCGPlayer, eBay, GameNerdz, Amazon.
  • Chase Magnitude — Higher top chase card value scores higher.
  • Pull Odds — Smaller “1 in N” rate scores higher. Community estimates from PokeBeach, CardChill.
  • Hit Density — More chase cards per total set size scores higher.

Data Sources

  • TCGPlayer — Sealed product market prices
  • eBay — 30-day median sold + active listings
  • Pokemon Center — MSRP + restock status
  • GameNerdz — Below-MSRP + Deal of the Day
  • Amazon — Lowest current offers
  • r/PokemonTCGDeals — Community-sourced deals
Pull rates are community estimates, not official data. Prices are volatile. This is a heuristic, not financial advice.
